# Final Project Pitch

## Project Name

RecallReady Intelligence

## One-Line Pitch

A Business Analyst case study that uses real NHTSA vehicle complaint and recall data to redesign how automotive service teams prioritize recurring vehicle issues.

## Interview Explanation

I wanted to build a Business Analyst project that was not just a dashboard. I used real public NHTSA vehicle complaint and recall data to study how automotive service teams could prioritize safety and repair issues. The project identifies recurring issue patterns by vehicle and component, then converts those patterns into a service priority queue, stakeholder workflow, business requirements, user stories, UAT cases, and an executive dashboard.

## Why It Is Business Analysis

The project connects data to business action:

Complaint/recall record -> issue pattern -> priority score -> owner team -> SLA -> recommended action -> future-state workflow

## Resume Bullet

Built RecallReady Intelligence, a Business Analyst case study using real NHTSA vehicle complaint and recall data to identify recurring service-risk patterns, prioritize issue queues, and design a complaint-to-service workflow with stakeholder mapping, business requirements, user stories, UAT scenarios, and an executive dashboard.
