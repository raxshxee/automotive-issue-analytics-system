# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | What They Care About | Dashboard / Workflow Need |
|---|---|---|
| Service Operations Manager | Which issues need action first | Priority queue, SLA, owner routing |
| Dealer Service Lead | What repairs/service issues to prepare for | Component patterns, recommended action |
| Product Quality Team | Recurring product or component issues | Complaint clusters, recall overlap |
| Customer Support Lead | How to route incoming complaints | Business rules and escalation logic |
| Compliance / Safety Team | Safety-sensitive issues and recall alignment | High-priority queue and evidence |
| Parts Planning Team | Whether parts may be needed | Component and recall pattern visibility |
| Executive Sponsor | Overall risk and operational health | KPI overview and trend patterns |
